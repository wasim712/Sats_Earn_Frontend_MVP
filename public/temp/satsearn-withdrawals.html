Step by Step
How to Withdraw
1
Reach your tier minimum
Keep earning until your available (matured) balance meets your tier's withdrawal minimum. Only matured sats count toward this — pending and maturing sats don't.
2
Open the withdrawal screen
From your dashboard, click Withdraw. You'll see your available balance and the exact fee for this withdrawal — no hidden charges.
3
Enter your Lightning address
Paste a Lightning invoice or LNURL from your Lightning wallet. Don't have one yet? Set one up first — it takes a couple of minutes.
4
Confirm and receive
Confirm the withdrawal. Sats typically arrive in your wallet within seconds to a few minutes. If a withdrawal ever fails, your sats are returned to your balance automatically.

New to Lightning?
Set Up a Wallet in 2 Minutes

Never used a Bitcoin wallet before? No problem. You only need one when you're ready to withdraw — and the simplest options take about two minutes to set up.

1
Download a wallet
Pick one of the Lightning wallets above and install it from your app store. Wallet of Satoshi or Blink are the easiest to start with — just open the app, no setup forms.
2
Find your receive address
Tap Receive in your wallet. You'll get either a Lightning address (looks like an email) or you can generate a Lightning invoice. Either one works for SatsEarn.
3
Withdraw from SatsEarn
Once your sats are matured and meet your tier minimum, paste your Lightning address or invoice into the withdrawal screen and confirm. Sats usually arrive in seconds.
Tip for total beginners: custodial wallets like Wallet of Satoshi and Blink are the quickest to start with. Self-custodial wallets (Phoenix, Muun, Breez) give you full control of your keys — a good step once you're comfortable. Both receive sats from SatsEarn the same way.
SatsEarn is not affiliated with any wallet provider. Wallet names are listed for convenience — always download wallets from official sources and keep your recovery phrase private.

Withdrawal Questions
Good to Know
Can I withdraw to an on-chain Bitcoin address?
Not currently — withdrawals are Lightning-only. On-chain fees make small sat amounts impractical. Many Lightning wallets let you swap to on-chain Bitcoin afterward if you need to.
Do pending or maturing sats count toward my minimum?
No. Only matured, available sats count toward your tier's withdrawal minimum. You can watch each entry's countdown in your dashboard as it moves from maturing to available.
What happens if my withdrawal fails?
Your sats return to your available balance automatically — nothing is lost. Common causes are an invalid Lightning address or an expired invoice. Just double-check and try again.
How do I lower my withdrawal minimum?
Upgrade to a paid tier. Minimums drop from 25,000 sats (free) down to 10,000 sats (Founders), and you earn more per task too — reaching payout far faster. See paid tiers →
