For Advertisers
How a Campaign Works
1
Tell us your goals
Share your product, your target audience, and what a successful campaign looks like — installs, sign-ups, in-app actions, or survey completions. We'll shape the right approach together.
2
Fund your campaign
Fund your budget in USD, USDT, or INR — whichever suits where you're paying from. We're based in India and work with advertisers globally. Your budget goes straight into the reward pool for your offer.
3
Your offer runs as a task
Your campaign appears to SatsEarn users as a rewarded task. They discover your product and complete the action you define — an install, a trial, a sign-up, or a survey.
4
Actions are verified
Every completion passes through our verification system before it counts. Combined with our 15-day maturity window, this filters out bots and fake activity — so you pay for genuine engagement, not noise.
5
Users earn real sats & you get results
Verified users are paid in Bitcoin via Lightning. You receive performance data on the actions that completed — so you can see what worked and decide how to scale.
