Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long do withdrawals take? ⌄
Once your balance has matured and you request a withdrawal, Lightning Network payouts are typically processed quickly — usually within minutes. If a withdrawal is pending much longer than expected, contact us at support@satsearn.app.
Why is my account restricted or suspended? ⌄
Accounts may be restricted for unusual activity or suspected multi-accounting. If you believe this is an error, email us with your registered email address and we'll investigate within 24–48 hours.
My task wasn't credited — what do I do? ⌄
Task credits go through a verification window before appearing as pending. If a completed task is still missing after that, make sure you finished all the required steps. Still missing? Email us with the task name, date, and a screenshot and we'll review it manually.
How do I cancel my paid tier? ⌄
You can manage your subscription from your account settings — cancelling stops future renewals while your access continues to the end of the current period. Note that all sales are final and subscription payments are non-refundable; see our Refund Policy for full details.
What is the minimum withdrawal amount? ⌄
Free tiers can withdraw from 25,000 sats. Paid tiers have lower minimums — from 20,000 sats (Platinum) down to 10,000 sats (Founders). See the Withdrawals page for the full breakdown by tier.
