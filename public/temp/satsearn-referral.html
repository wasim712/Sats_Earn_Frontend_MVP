A Bit of History

It started with a faucet

June 2010
The Genesis Faucet
  • Developer Gavin Andresen launched the first Bitcoin faucet.
  • Solve a captcha, paste an address → receive 5 BTC free.
  • He seeded it with 1,100 BTC of his own to onboard newcomers.
~5¢ per BTC then
May 2010
Pizza Day
  • Laszlo Hanyecz bought two pizzas for 10,000 BTC — Bitcoin's first real-world purchase.
  • Worth about $41 at the time; it proved Bitcoin could buy real goods.
First commercial BTC use
2011–2013
The Faucet Boom
  • Hundreds of faucets appeared as Bitcoin spread worldwide.
  • Per-visit payouts shrank from whole coins to tiny fractions.
  • Faucets stayed the main on-ramp for non-technical newcomers.
100s of faucets
2014–2017
Scaling Broke It
  • Network congestion pushed on-chain fees to $5–$50+ per send.
  • Sending someone a few sats cost more than the sats were worth.
  • Most classic faucets quietly shut down.
Fees > payouts
2018
Lightning Arrives
  • The Lightning Network brought instant, near-free Bitcoin payments.
  • Fees drop below a single satoshi; settlement is sub-second.
  • Sending 1 sat becomes practical again — micro-earning is back.
<1 sat fees
2026
The SatsEarn Era
  • The same spirit — earn Bitcoin without buying — rebuilt Lightning-native.
  • Earn real sats via tasks, quizzes, streaks, games & referrals.
  • Paid straight to your Lightning wallet — no buying required.

Referral FAQ

Common referral questions

The things people ask most before sharing their link.

Where is my referral link and how does attribution work?+
Every account gets one unique referral link in your dashboard. When someone opens it and creates an account, they're permanently attributed to you. Share the link and any signups through it are tracked to you automatically — nothing to set up.
How is my commission calculated?+
Commission is a percentage of the admin-set base reward for each task your referral completes — set by your tier, not theirs. Example at a 10-sat base reward: Platinum (10%) earns 1 sat per task; Founders (30%) earns 3 sats. You only earn when your referral is genuinely active, not just for signing up.
When is referral commission paid out?+
It follows the same flow as all earned sats: a 24-hour verification window (Pending), then a 15-day maturity lock (Maturing) with its own countdown, then it becomes Available to withdraw. Each entry matures independently, so commission unlocks on a rolling basis.
Is there a cap on referral earnings?+
Free tiers have cumulative lifetime caps (Basic 20 referrals / 2,000 sats, up to Gold 100 referrals / 10,000 sats). All paid tiers — Platinum through Founders — are uncapped: unlimited referrals, no earnings ceiling.
Do my referrals or my link expire?+
No. Your link doesn't expire and a referral stays attributed to you for as long as they use SatsEarn. On a paid tier you keep earning on their activity indefinitely; on a free tier, until you hit your lifetime cap.
Can I refer myself or family?+
You can refer genuine people — real friends and family with their own accounts and activity. Self-referral is not allowed: extra accounts for yourself, bots, or fake accounts are treated as abuse and result in forfeiture of affected balances and possible account action.
