The Game

Grow long,
survive longer

SAT WORM takes the snake formula everyone knows and wires it into SatsEarn. You steer a glowing worm around a 24×24 grid, eating lightning-bolt "sat food" to grow and push your score higher.

The catch: the further you get, the more the board fights back. Bombs, timed explosives, walls, and fake food unlock in stages — and one wrong move ends the run.

  • Every bolt eaten = +10 points and one segment longer.
  • Cross 100 points to bank XP for the run.
  • Reach 1,000 points to max out a run.
🎯 Objective

Control the worm to collect sat food. Every bolt you eat grows the worm and adds 10 points. Reach 1,000 points for maximum XP — and whatever you do, don't crash. Hitting a wall, a bomb, fake food, or your own tail ends the run instantly.

Game Elements

what's on the board

Six things share the grid with you. Hazards aren't there from the start — they unlock as you eat more food.

⚡
Sat Food
The bolt you're chasing. Eat it to score and grow. It carries a countdown ring — if the ring empties before you reach it, the food teleports to a new spot.

A decoy that looks edible. Marked by a purple dashed ring and a mushroom icon. Touching it is instant death — real food is always orange.

Bomb
A static obstacle that sits on the grid. Run into it and the run is over. Only one can be active at a time.
Unlocks at food #10

Timed Bomb
Counts down and detonates after 6 seconds. It blinks red as it nears explosion and can kill you on an adjacent tile, not just a direct hit.
Unlocks at food #15
🧱
Wall Block
A solid barrier formation. Starts as a single tile and grows to 2, then 3 connected tiles deeper into a run. Only one formation exists at a time.
Unlocks at food #20
🐍
Your Own Tail
As you grow, you become your own biggest hazard. Double back into any segment of your body and you die instantly.
Always active
In Motion

Read the board, plan the route

The worm's head glows brightest and carries the direction it's travelling. Behind it, the body fades from bright orange to deep amber so you can always trace your tail.

The sat food pulses with a countdown ring that drains clockwise — orange means you have time, yellow means hurry, red means it's about to teleport. Good players don't chase; they cut the food off.

Active play. The worm tracks toward sat food with its draining countdown ring.
Difficulty Progression

How the pressure ramps

SAT WORM eases you in, then unlocks hazards in stages tied to how many bolts you've eaten. Speed steps up at the same milestones.

FOOD 1–9 · WARM-UP
A slow start with no hazards at all. Use this window to learn the controls and build some length and room to manoeuvre.
FOOD 10+ · BOMBS APPEAR
The first static bomb unlocks. From here, speed steps up as you cross major food milestones rather than after every bite.
FOOD 15+ · TIMED BOMBS
Timed bombs join the board. They detonate after 6 seconds and can catch you on an adjacent tile — move clear the moment they blink red.
FOOD 20+ · WALLS
Walls unlock as a single-tile formation. Only one formation exists at once, but it reshapes the safe space you have to work with.
FOOD 25+ · FAKE FOOD
Fake food unlocks — a purple-ringed decoy that kills on contact. Now every bolt you approach needs a second look.
FOOD 30 / 40 / 50+ · MAX DANGER
Walls grow to 2 tiles at 30+ and 3 tiles at 40+. From 50+, every unlocked hazard can reappear at random, one active instance per type. This is the deep end.

Pro Tips

Survive longer, score more

A handful of habits separate a short run from a deep one.

Build room early. The first nine bolts have no hazards — use them to grow and learn the speed before anything dangerous spawns.
Watch the food ring. The arc drains clockwise. Orange is safe, yellow means hurry, red means it's about to teleport — commit to your route before it resets.
Real food is always orange. Once fake food unlocks at #25, check the ring: a purple dashed ring and a mushroom means instant death. Steer away.
Timed bombs kill at range. When one blinks red it can take you out on an adjacent tile, not just a direct hit. Clear the area early.
Mind the growing walls. Walls get bigger past food 30 and 40. Keep a mental map of the open lanes so a new formation doesn't trap you.
Use the revive wisely. After a crash you may get one continue (it costs points). Bank it for a deep run rather than an early stumble.
