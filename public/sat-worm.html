⚡ HOW TO PLAY
  SAT—WORM · SATSEARN MINI-GAME
🎯 OBJECTIVE
Control the worm to collect ⚡ Sat food. Every food you eat grows the worm and increases your score by 10 points. Reach 1000 points to max out your sat earnings. Don't crash — ever.
🗺 GAME ELEMENTS
⚡
SAT FOOD
Collect these to score. Has a countdown ring — eat it before the ring runs out or it teleports!
🍄
FAKE FOOD
Looks edible, it's a trap. Purple ring = instant death.
💣
BOMB
Static obstacle. Touch it and you're done. Unlocks at food #10. Only one can be active at a time.
⏰
TIMED BOMB
Counts down and explodes. Blinks red when close to detonation.
🧱
WALL BLOCK
Static barrier formation. Unlocks at food #20 and grows from 1 tile to 3 connected tiles as the run advances.
🐍
YOURSELF
Don't double back into your own tail — you will die instantly.
📈 DIFFICULTY PROGRESSION
1
Food 1–9: Slow start, no hazards. Learn the controls and build room.
2
Food 10+: Static 💣 bomb unlocks. Speed only changes when you cross major food milestones.
3
Food 15+: ⏰ Timed bomb unlocks. It explodes after 6 seconds and can kill on adjacent tiles.
4
Food 20+: 🧱 Walls unlock as a single-tile formation. Only one wall formation can exist at once.
5
Food 25+: 🍄 Fake food unlocks. Purple ring means instant death if you touch it.
6
Food 30+ / 40+ / 50+: Walls grow to 2 tiles at 30+, 3 tiles at 40+, and from 50+ all unlocked hazards can reappear randomly with one active instance per type.
🎮 CONTROLS
⌨️

💡 PRO TIPS
⚡ Watch the food ring — the arc depletes clockwise. Early in the game you have plenty of time; as difficulty rises the window shrinks. Orange arc = safe, yellow = hurry, red = almost gone — move immediately.
🍄 Identify fake food — it has a purple dashed ring and a mushroom icon. Real food is always orange.
💣 Timed bombs blink red when about to explode. They can kill you if you're on an adjacent tile — get clear fast.
🧱 Hug the open space — walls and your own tail shrink the safe area. Plan your route, don't just react.
🏆 XP is capped — once you reach 100 score, you earn the full 5 XP for that run. Higher score does not increase XP further.
